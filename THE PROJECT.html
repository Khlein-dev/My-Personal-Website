FEATURED EVENTS AND

HIGHLIGHTS

Beautiful Baguio

I will always miss my trip and experience in Baguio. The cold breeze, the beautiful sceneries. It's perfect. An experience I will never forget. June 16th 2024. I was with my co workers for a vacation trip, where we enjoy ourselves and bond together.

Baguio is a breathtaking gem nestled in the mountains, where pine-scented air, rolling hills, and vibrant blooms create an enchanting atmosphere. The city's cool climate, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality make it a perfect retreat, whether you're strolling through Burnham Park, marveling at the panoramic views from Mines View Park, or indulging in local delicacies at the bustling markets. Every corner of Baguio is a masterpiece of nature and culture, making it an unforgettable destination.

Majestic La Union

The beach experiences I had are always great, but nothing can compare what I had when I was in La Union Beach. It was soothing and relaxing. If I had given a chance to experience it over again, I would in a heartbeat. This one of my memorable experience in 2024.

La Union Beach is a tropical paradise where golden sands meet the rolling waves of the West Philippine Sea. Known as the surfing capital of the North, its breathtaking sunsets, laid-back vibes, and vibrant beach culture make it a haven for adventure seekers and chill travelers alike. Whether you're riding the waves, enjoying fresh seafood by the shore, or simply soaking in the salty breeze, La Union offers a perfect escape where nature’s beauty and coastal charm come together in harmony.
